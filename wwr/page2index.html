Lotus, California White Water Rafting Co. Prices, Dates, and Itinerary

Adult Prices:

Half-day: $105 Full-day: $175 Trip Of 3-Nights: $525 per adult Trip Of 6-Nights: $1050 per adult

Itineraries:

Half-day excursions include loading the raft, gearing up, and going about 10 miles down the river to a stopping destination where all clients are offered a ride in the Lotus, California White Water Rafting Co. van back to the starting destination point of the rafting excursion where client vehicles are parked. Full-day excursions include loading the raft, gearing up, and going about 10 miles down the river. Then stopping on the side of the river for lunch. After lunch clients are encouraged to join the 1.5 round trip mile hike before getting back into the raft and going a little further down the Lotus California river to the end designation point where all clients are offered a ride in the Lotus California White Water Rafting Co. van back to the starting destination point of the rafting excursion where client vehicles are parked. For a 3-night trip, the excursion includes loading the raft, gearing up, and going down the river about 25 miles on the first day. At about 17 miles down the river lunch will be provided. The rafting group will be guided to the camping destination point to set up camp for the night by the rafting tour guide. On the second day after camp is packed up, the raft is loaded, and everyone is geared up the raft tour guide will guide the group down the river about 5 more miles before stopping on the side of the river for everyone to have lunch and take a 1.5 round trip mile hike. Afterward in the same location set up camp for the night and take it down the next morning to load the raft, get geared up, and raft about 10 more miles down the river to the end destination point for the 3-night trip. At the end destination point, all clients are offered a ride in the Lotus, California White Water Rafting Co. van back to the starting destination point of the rafting excursion where client vehicles are parked. For a 6-night trip, the excursion includes loading the raft, gearing up, and going down the river about 25 miles on the first day. Then stopping at the camping destination point to set up camp for the night. On the second day after camp is packed up, the raft is loaded, and everyone is geared up a raft tour guide will guide the group down the river about 24 miles to the next camping destination point for the night. On the third day, there will be a morning 1.5 round trip mile hike into the beautiful dirt trails near the Lotus California river. Once back from the hike camp is taken down and rafting is continued about 10 more miles down the river to the next camping destination point. At the camping destination point, the task of setting up camp takes place for the night. The fourth day is mostly just full of white water rafting for about another 25 miles down the river to the camping destination point for the night. The next day on the morning of the fifth day of rafting camp is packed up, the raft is loaded, and everyone is geared up for the tour guide to have everyone raft about 7 more miles down the river to the last camping destination point where there is a nearby lake. Once everyone unloads from the raft and sets up camp the rest of the day is free for clients to explore, hike, swim, fish, and relax. In the early morning of the sixth-day camp is taken down, the raft is loaded, and everyone gears up to continue white water rafting about 15 more miles down the river to the end destination point. At the end destination point, all clients are offered a ride in the Lotus, California White Water Rafting Co. van back to the starting destination point of the rafting excursion where client vehicles are parked. 19 Day trips, 3-night trips, and 6-night trips offer opportunities for fishing. Just request for fishing gear to be included unless you plan to bring your own when scheduling a day trip, 3-night trip, and/or 6-night trip. Meals are included in the prices listed for 3 and 6 night trips unless you have dietary restrictions please bring your own food. Contact Us for reservations and any questions you may have.
