Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to have a space where customers may reserve available dates of trips down the Lotus River of El Dorado County in California with us the Lotus, California White Water Rafting Co. on the internet. By having a website, I as a owner can better advertise my business and knowledge about what my company has to offer as a white-water rafting company of the beautiful Lotus California River.

Audience

This website is for those individuals that are interested in white water rafting on the Lotus River in California. Whether you want to try something new or are an experienced white water rafter the Lotus, California White Water Rafting Co. is a great choice as a company to go white water rafting with. This website is for those that are interested in white water rafting with the Lotus, California White Water Rafting Co.. Lotus, California White Water Rafting Co. offers white water rafting for people ages 10 and up. White water rafting is a good way to fulfill a person's desire for adventure, get that rush of adrenaline, feel the sun on your skin, smell that fresh mountain air, and create lasting positive memories with new friends, old friends, acquaintances, and/or family. A way to get away from the city, work, roads, and social media. An experience where you get to see the stars at night, converse with others, be outdoors, eat good food, and be on the water. This website was built to be accessible by desktops and laptops.
